  PRESENT RESEARCH STAFF                                    
 

      

Qiang Zeng

Dr. Zeng joined us as a Research Assistant since July 2018. He is currently also an Assistant Professor at the South China University of Technology, Guangzhou, China.
 

      

Wenbo Fan

Dr. Fan joined us as a Research Associate to conduct transit network design research. Dr. Fan's research interests also include transit-oriented development, advanced/revised congestion pricing, and tradable mobility credits in congestion management. Prior to working in HK PolyU, he conducted statewide transportation modelling in NCSG (National Center for Smart Growth Research and Education), University of Maryland in 2015. He is currently an Associate Professor at the Southwest Jiaotong University in China. Dr. Fan has published more than 40 research papers in the field of transportation demand management, of which 10 papers were published on SSCI/SCI indexed Journals, or presented at Annual Meetings of TRB and INFORMS. He is also a committee board member of Modern Traffic and Transportation Engineering Research.
